Noodles recipe

Description:

A quick and tasty stir-fry made with boiled noodles, crunchy vegetables, and flavorful sauces. Perfect for a light meal or evening snack.

  1. Ingredients (2 servings):

    • 150 g (about 1 packet) noodles
    • 1 small onion (sliced)
    • ½ capsicum (sliced)
    • 1 carrot (thinly sliced or julienned)
    • ¼ cup cabbage (shredded, optional)
    • 2–3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
    • 1–2 green chilies (optional)
    • 1–2 tsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp vinegar
    • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
    • 1–2 tbsp oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  2. Steps:

    1. Cook noodles:
      • Boil noodles in salted water till 90% cooked (not mushy).
      • Drain and toss with a few drops of oil to prevent sticking.
    2. Stir-fry veggies:
      • Heat oil in a wok/pan on high flame.
      • Add garlic (and green chilies if using), sauté for a few seconds.
      • Add onion, capsicum, carrot, cabbage – stir-fry 2–3 minutes (keep them crunchy).
    3. Add sauces:
      • Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce. Mix well.
    4. Mix noodles:
      • Add boiled noodles, toss on high heat so they absorb the flavors.
      • Sprinkle salt and pepper. Mix well.

Your hot & tasty Veg Noodles are ready in about 15 minutes!
