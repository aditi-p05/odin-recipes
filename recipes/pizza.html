Pizza recipe

Description:

A simple and delicious homemade pizza made with a soft base, tangy pizza sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and a mix of fresh vegetables like capsicum, onion, tomato, and sweet corn. Baked to perfection, this cheesy delight is easy to prepare and perfect for a quick snack or dinner.

  1. Ingredients:

    • For the base (or use ready-made pizza base):

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
    • ½ tsp instant yeast (or 1 tsp dry yeast)
    • ½ tsp sugar
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • 1-2 tsp oil
    • Warm water (as needed)

    • For the base toppings:

    • 3-4 tbsp pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade with tomato puree, garlic, oregano, salt, sugar)
    • ½ cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
    • ½ cup mixed vegetables (capsicum, onion, tomato, sweet corn, mushroom, olives – your choice)
    • A pinch of oregano/chili flakes

  2. Steps:

    1. Make the dough (skip if using ready-made base):

      1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
      2. Add warm water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
      3. Add oil, knead again, cover, and rest for 1–2 hours (till dough doubles in size).

    2. Prepare the base:

      1. Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F).
      2. out the dough into a round (not too thick).
      3. Place on a greased baking tray.

    3. Assemble the pizza:

      1. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
      2. Sprinkle some cheese.
      3. Add vegetables of choice.
      4. Top with more cheese.
      5. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.

    4. Bake:

      • Bake for 12–15 minutes at 200°C until the cheese melts and edges turn golden brown.

    Your homemade Veg Pizza is ready!
